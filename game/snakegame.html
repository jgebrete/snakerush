SNAKE RUSH

How to Play:

Goal: Move your snake towards the food item on the map and eat as much as you can to grow your snake as long as you can. Be careful not to bump your head into the walls surrounding the map though!

Controls: If you're playing on computer, you can use your arrow keys to move the snake around the map and towards your meal. If you're on mobile, you can tap the onscreen buttons to navigate!

How To Win: As your snake continues to eat and grow, your speed will accelerate, so keep those reflexes sharp to ensure your survival! Rounds will begin based on how much your snake has grown from eating (i.e. Every 20 meals progresses you to the next round). If you survive all of the rounds, you win!
